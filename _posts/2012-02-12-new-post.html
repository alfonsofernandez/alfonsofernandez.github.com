I was trying to get Octopress working on a Mac OS 10.7.3 with XCode 4.3.
I found this great resource:
http://aralbalkan.com/3963/comment-page-1#comment-265675
But bundle install fails with XCode 4.3
You can find the reason here
https://github.com/thibaudgg/rb-fsevent/issues/26 And fix it with
sudo xcode-select -switch /Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/Developer/Platforms/MacOSX.platform/Developer/
